GROUP INCOME

Basic Income for your friends and family.

Group income makes it easy for small communities to voluntarily provide a financial safety net for their members.

Get started
What is Group Income?

A simple way to encourage basic financial security.

The goal of Basic Income is to guarantee that everyone gets access to enough money to meet their fundamental needs, independent of their life circumstances.

Learn more about Basic Income
Why it matters?

Every individual is valuable.

Many people contribute to our lives and our society, but are often not financially rewarded. From students to unpaid careers, from volunteers to care givers, from unemployed people to unwell.
Basic Income can improve the quality of life for millions of people, simply through providing them with basic financial security and the peace of mind that goes with it.

Mincome

The minimum amount each one should receive.

The mincome is the agreed value that a group sets as the amount each member needs to live a productive and healthy life. Each month, group members contribute to help those who haven't crossed the mincome threshold.

With Group Income

Share your income with your friends and family.

Create stronger communities and ensure basic financial security for the people you care about.

Contribute

Each member can contribute to the group.

Members of the group can contribute with monetary or non-monetary donations, securing everyone in your group with an basic income, independet of employment.
